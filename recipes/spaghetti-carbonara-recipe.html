Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is an Italian classic that's as easy to make as it is delicious. Originating from Rome, this dish is a go-to recipe for pasta lovers. Made with simple ingredients like spaghetti, pancetta, eggs, and cheese, it offers a creamy, flavorful experience without the use of any cream. The freshly ground black pepper and parsley add the perfect finishing touches. It's the ideal dish for a quick weeknight dinner or an elegant date night at home.

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Small bunch of fresh flat-leaf parsley

Steps:

  1. Cook spaghetti in a pot of boiling salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta with garlic until crispy, then remove garlic.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, pecorino, and parmesan cheeses.
  4. Add black pepper to the egg mixture.
  5. Drain the cooked spaghetti and add it to the pan with pancetta.
  6. Remove from heat and add the egg mixture. Stir quickly to avoid scrambling.
  7. Garnish with parsley and additional parmesan before serving.
